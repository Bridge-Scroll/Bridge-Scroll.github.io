L2 Rollup Offloading

To decrease transaction fees, the system routes paths through Layer-2 chains (such as Arbitrum scaling or Optimism). L2 scaling chains execute batches off-chain and submit state updates to Layer-1, reducing fee costs by over 90% while retaining L1 security standards tracked on L2BEAT metrics.
