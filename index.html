What is Bridge Scroll?

Scroll Bridge is the Scroll portal flow for moving supported assets between Ethereum mainnet and Scroll, a native zkEVM Layer 2 for Ethereum.

How Bridge Scroll works

  1. Open the portalStart at the official app URL and confirm the portal.scroll.io domain before connecting a wallet.
  2. Connect walletUse a wallet that supports Ethereum and Scroll, then approve only the network prompts you expect.
  3. Select routeChoose the asset, direction, and amount shown in the portal; keep gas available on the source network.
  4. Review and signCheck the wallet confirmation, submit the transaction, and track progress from your wallet or explorer.

Bridge Scroll FAQ

What is Bridge Scroll?

Bridge Scroll is an app-first landing page that points users to the official Scroll bridge portal for moving supported assets between Ethereum and Scroll.

Is Bridge Scroll the official Scroll app?

No. The official action should happen at portal.scroll.io/bridge; always verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

What can I move with Bridge Scroll?

Use the official portal to see the currently supported assets and routes. Availability can change, so rely on the app rather than a static token list.

How long does Bridge Scroll take?

Timing depends on the route, network conditions, and the portal flow. Review the app status and wallet transaction details before assuming settlement is complete.

Where can I read more before using Bridge Scroll?

Start with the Scroll bridge docs and compare the instructions with the app screen before signing.
