Why bridge here

Bridge Scroll without handing over custody

Scroll bridging is about getting capital where it can work: ETH and supported tokens on a lower-cost rollup environment, while your wallet stays in control of every approval and transaction.

Your wallet stays in charge

Bridge from a browser wallet or mobile wallet, review the request, and sign only when the route matches what you want. A self-custody wallet keeps private keys with you.

Built around Ethereum assets

Start with ETH and supported ERC-token routes from the Ethereum settlement layer, then arrive on Scroll ready for apps, swaps and DeFi positions.

Cost and timing before confirmation

Bridge Scroll with the live network conditions in view. Gas, route details and expected completion timing appear before signing, so you are not guessing at the final step.

How it works

Bridge to Scroll in three steps

The flow is direct: connect, choose the route, then confirm from your wallet after reviewing the live details.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect the wallet holding the asset you want to bridge. The app does not take custody of your funds.

2

Pick asset and destination

Select ETH or another supported asset, choose Scroll as the destination, and let the app prepare the bridge route.

3

Review and sign

Check gas, route, token details and estimated timing in the app, then approve and confirm from your wallet.

FAQ

Bridge Scroll questions that matter before you sign

01

What is Bridge Scroll?

Bridge Scroll is a non-custodial DeFi app for moving crypto assets onto Scroll. It prepares the route, shows the live quote in the app, and lets you confirm from your own wallet.

02

Which chains and assets can I use?

The app focuses on Ethereum-to-Scroll routes and supported wallet assets such as ETH and listed ERC-token options. See the current network list on the supported chains page.

03

How long does a Scroll bridge take?

Bridge timing depends on the source chain, destination, route and network conditions. The app shows the current estimate before you approve the transaction.

04

What does it cost to bridge to Scroll?

Cost depends on gas, the selected route and any bridge-related execution costs. The app displays the live cost details before you sign.

05

Is Bridge Scroll non-custodial?

Yes. You connect a wallet and sign transactions yourself. Bridge Scroll does not ask you to deposit into an account or give up control of your private keys.

06

Why bridge assets to Scroll?

Scroll is an EVM-compatible rollup ecosystem for DeFi activity. Independent rollup research can help users understand how layer-two networks compare before moving funds.
